Quick-draw Cowboy

Are you the fastest draw on the web? Find out with Quick-draw Cowboy and reenact the iconic duals from Westerns!

How to play

1. Begin the game.

To start the game move your right hand to the holstered position. This will start a random timer to test your reaction skills.

2. Draw your revolver

When the timer finishes, "DRAW" will appear. Draw your revolver as shown in the image above.

3. FIRE!

After drawing your revolver, FIRE! The game will show how long it took for you to draw and fire your gun. To start again simply move your hand back to the 1st position.
